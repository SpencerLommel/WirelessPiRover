Pin 4 (5v Power): 5v OUT+ on first voltage stepdown converter
Pin 6 (Ground): OUT- on first voltage stepdown converter
Pin 16 (GPIO 23): IN1 on L298n Motor Driver
Pin 18 (GPIO 24): IN2 on L298n Motor Driver
Pin 22 (GPIO 25): First ENA Pin (Closest to edge) on L298n Motor Driver
